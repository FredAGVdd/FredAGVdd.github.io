When I started computing, hardware was everything - Commodore 64 / IBM PC 8088. Software was what we were writing.

Following years, computers became more powerful (progression) - and I slowly lost interest in hardware.

Discovering that VMWare needs certain CPU models to function properly made me realize this lack of interest in (every so quickly changing) hardware needs changing.

Then I stumbled across these: Personal Computer shifts to Mini Computer ?

- http://www.solid-run.com/products/cubox
