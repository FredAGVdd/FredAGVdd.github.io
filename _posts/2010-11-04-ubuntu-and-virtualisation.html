It's always nice to catch up with a few things one has been delaying for (say) weeks...

I'm impressed with the easy and quality of the free software available to virtualise computers!

In case you're new to virtualisation of computers; roughly speaking, a virtual computer is 'software behaving like a full computer'.

When your home computer is about to die, or you have the extra $$ to buy a more powerfull machine, then you can move your virtual computer by 'copy and pasting' a folder (kinda)...

I'm also using virutalisation to run Ubuntu in Windows and Windows in Ubuntu - now how silly is that ;-)
