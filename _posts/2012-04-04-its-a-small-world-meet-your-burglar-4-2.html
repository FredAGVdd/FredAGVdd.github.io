Yesterday, I was sat on the 222 bus to Uxbridge with ... the fella that burgled my home last year.

Obviously, I would not know this fella, if

- he had not left his fingerprint on a (only!) half-finished bottle of Leffe bruin
- the Crown Prosecution Service did not take him to Harrow Crown Court
- I did not go testify in this case ...

Obviously, all of the above DID happen :-)

I can put a smiley now, but there and then I did feel a little intimidated. Where it not for his gentle persistent approach, we would not have spoken! (I would have kept wondering whom I saw on the bus...) But now, it's all so much clearer, that grey sky :-) The only thing is, I'm still short two laptops, one of which has months of tedious configuring stuff on it ...
